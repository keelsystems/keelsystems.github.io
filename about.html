About Keel Systems

Born on the rugged coast of Newfoundland, Keel Systems is rooted in practicality and community. We believe in equipping businesses with robust, transparent technology—so they stay resilient in any weather.

Our Mission

To deliver turnkey, open‑source‑driven IT solutions that empower clients with control, clarity, and confidence.

Values

  • Empowerment – We document everything so clients are self‑sufficient.
  • Balance – We automate the boring stuff so people can focus on what matters.
  • Integrity – No upsells, no vendor lock‑in. Just honest advice.

Meet the Founder

Jonathan Thoms is an IT manager, maker, and dad who’s passionate about open‑source tech and helping others. After overseeing complex IT rebuilds at Canada Fluorspar, he launched Keel Systems to share that expertise with businesses across the Burin Peninsula and beyond.
