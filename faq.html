Frequently Asked Questions

Do you only use open‑source?

Open‑source is our default, but we recommend commercial gear when it solves a problem better or meets compliance requirements.

What regions do you serve?

We’re based in Newfoundland and serve the Burin, Clarenville, and Carbonear areas on‑site, with remote support available Canada‑wide.

Starter projects are often complete within two weeks; larger enterprise builds can span 4–8 weeks plus follow‑up audits.
